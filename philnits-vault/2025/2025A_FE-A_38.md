# 2025A_FE-A_38 %% ex. 2025A_FE-A_83 %%

What is the expected value in dollars for the correction cost of an application program in the first year?

**Conditions:**
1. Program size: 2,000 k steps
2. Potential defective rate: 0.04 defects/k steps
3. Annual discovery rate: 20%/year
4. Major impact: 20%, Minor impact: 80%
5. Correction cost: Major = $20,000, Minor = $5,000
6. In the first year, only **major impact** defects are corrected

a) 64,000
b) 128,000
c) 160,000
d) 640,000
?
a) 64,000
### Explanation

Step-by-step:
1. **Total potential defects**: $2000 \times 0.04 = 80$ defects
2. **Discovered in year 1**: $80 \times 20\% = 16$ defects
3. **Major impact defects**: $16 \times 20\% = 3.2$ defects
4. **Correction cost**: $3.2 \times \$20,000 = \mathbf{\$64,000}$

Only major impact defects are corrected in year 1, so minor defect costs are not included.
