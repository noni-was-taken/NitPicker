# 2025A_FE-A_46 %% ex. 2025A_FE-A_83 %%

Which of the following is an appropriate feasibility evaluation when checking whether the lifetime benefits of the proposed information system are greater than its lifetime costs?

a) Economic feasibility
b) Operational feasibility
c) Scheduling feasibility
d) Technical feasibility
?
a) Economic feasibility
### Explanation

**Economic feasibility** evaluates whether the financial benefits of a proposed system (ROI, cost savings, revenue generation over its lifetime) outweigh its total costs (development, operation, maintenance). This is essentially a cost-benefit analysis.

- **Operational feasibility**: assesses whether the system will be accepted and used effectively by people
- **Scheduling feasibility**: assesses whether the project can be completed within the available timeframe
- **Technical feasibility**: assesses whether the required technology and expertise exist to build the system
