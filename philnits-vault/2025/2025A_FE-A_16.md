# 2025A_FE-A_16 %% ex. 2025A_FE-S_83 %%

Which of the following is equivalent to the logical circuit shown below?

[2025A_FE-A_16.png]

a) $\overline{A \cdot B}$
b) $\overline{A} \cdot \overline{B}$
c) $\overline{A} + \overline{B}$
d) $A \cdot B$
?
c) $\overline{A} + \overline{B}$
### Step 1: Identify the circuit

The circuit shown is a NAND gate configuration that implements the logical expression:

$$\overline{A \cdot B}$$

### Step 2: Apply De Morgan's Law

By De Morgan's theorem:

$$\overline{A \cdot B} = \overline{A} + \overline{B}$$

This means the output of a NAND gate on inputs A and B equals NOT-A OR NOT-B.

### Step 3: Match to options

Option c) $\overline{A} + \overline{B}$ matches De Morgan's expansion of NAND.

### Answer: c) $\overline{A} + \overline{B}$
