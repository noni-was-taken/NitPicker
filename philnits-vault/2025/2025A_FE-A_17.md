# 2025A_FE-A_17 %% ex. 2025A_FE-A_83 %%

Which of the following is a method used to improve the appearance of a jagged edge of a slanted line so that it appears smooth on a screen, such as that of an LCD?

a) Anti-aliasing
b) Bump mapping
c) Shading
d) Texture mapping
?
a) Anti-aliasing
### Explanation

**Anti-aliasing** is a technique that smooths the jagged, staircase-like edges (called "jaggies" or aliasing artifacts) that appear on diagonal or curved lines on raster displays. It works by blending the colors of edge pixels with the background to create a smoother visual transition.

- **Bump mapping**: simulates surface roughness/texture using normals
- **Shading**: determines the color/lighting of a 3D surface
- **Texture mapping**: wraps a 2D image onto a 3D surface
