# 2025A_FE-A_52
Which of the following is the appropriate description of process innovation?

a) Development of an innovative new product on the basis of an original and high technology
b) Development of innovative manufacturing procedures that improve the quality of a product
c) Outsourcing manufacturing to another company that has a semiconductor manufacturing process
d) Wide adoption after passing the competition, resulting in the establishment of a de facto standard
?
b) Development of innovative manufacturing procedures that improve the quality of a product
### Explanation

**Process innovation** refers to the implementation of new or significantly improved **production or delivery methods**. This includes changes in techniques, equipment, or software that result in better quality, efficiency, or reduced costs in how a product or service is made.

- Option a) describes **product innovation**
- Option c) describes **outsourcing / manufacturing strategy**, not innovation
- Option d) describes the establishment of a **de facto standard** through market competition
