# 2025A_FE-A_49
Which of the following is an explanation of benchmarking that is used for corporate management?

a) It refers to a qualitative and quantitative understanding of a company's own products, services, and operations through comparison with those of competitors or advanced companies.
b) It refers to drastically reforming the quality and structure of a company by redesigning its business processes from a customer viewpoint and by taking full advantage of information technology.
c) It refers to the ability to plan and manage the allocation of company-wide management resources in an effective and integrated manner in order to improve management efficiency.
d) It refers to the concentration of management resources on the unique skills and technologies of a company that can generate profit and that are superior to those of other companies.
?
a) It refers to a qualitative and quantitative understanding of a company's own products, services, and operations through comparison with those of competitors or advanced companies.
### Explanation

**Benchmarking** in corporate management is the practice of comparing a company's products, services, processes, or performance metrics against those of industry leaders or competitors in order to identify gaps and best practices for improvement.

- Option b) describes **BPR (Business Process Reengineering)**
- Option c) describes **ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning)** or integrated resource management
- Option d) describes **core competency strategy**
