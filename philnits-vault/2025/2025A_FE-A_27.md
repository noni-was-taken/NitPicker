# 2025A_FE-A_27 %% ex. 2025A_FE-A_83 %%

An attacker left a USB stick labeled 'Next ITPEC exam questions and answers' that contains malware in front of an office, expecting someone to plug it into a PC. Once plugged in, the attacker gains control of the PC. Which of the following types of attack is this?

a) Baiting
b) Identity theft
c) Phishing
d) Pretexting
?
a) Baiting
### Explanation

**Baiting** is a social engineering attack where the attacker lures a victim using a tempting physical item (like a USB stick with an enticing label) to get them to compromise their own security. The victim's curiosity or greed drives them to plug in the device.

- **Identity theft**: stealing someone's personal information to impersonate them
- **Phishing**: deceiving users via fraudulent emails or websites to obtain credentials
- **Pretexting**: creating a fabricated scenario to manipulate someone into revealing information
