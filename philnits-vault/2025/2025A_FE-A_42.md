# 2025A_FE-A_42 %% ex. 2025A_FE-A_83 %%

Based on the requirements gathered, the cost of a software project under normal conditions is estimated at $70,000. In smooth implementation, the cost is $50,000; in the worst case, the cost is $120,000. What is the expected cost calculated using the three-point estimate (PERT beta distribution)?

a) 70,000
b) 75,000
c) 80,000
d) 85,000
?
b) 75,000
### Explanation

The **PERT three-point estimate** formula using beta distribution is:

$$\text{Expected Cost} = \frac{\text{Optimistic} + 4 \times \text{Most Likely} + \text{Pessimistic}}{6}$$

$$= \frac{50,000 + 4 \times 70,000 + 120,000}{6} = \frac{50,000 + 280,000 + 120,000}{6} = \frac{450,000}{6} = \mathbf{\$75,000}$$
