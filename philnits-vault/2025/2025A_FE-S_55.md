# 2025A_FE-S_55
Which of the following corresponds to (5) in the example of a model of an affiliate program that is shown in the Internet advertising? Here, (1) to (5) indicate the order of operations in the model, and (1), (2), (4), and (5) each correspond to one of the options a) to d).

[2025A_FE-S_55.png]

a) Agree to and participate in the affiliate program
b) Click the advertisement on the website
c) Pay a success fee on the basis of the sales of the product
d) Purchase the desired product
?
c) Pay a success fee on the basis of the sales of the product
### Step 1: Understand the affiliate program model

An affiliate program works as follows:
1. **(1)** Advertiser and affiliate (website owner) **agree to and participate** in the affiliate program.
2. **(2)** Affiliate places an advertisement on their website → user **clicks**.
3. **(3)** User is redirected to the advertiser's website (system/tracking step — not in the options).
4. **(4)** User **purchases the desired product** from the advertiser.
5. **(5)** Advertiser **pays a success fee** to the affiliate based on the completed sale.

### Step 2: Identify step (5)

Step (5) is the final step where the advertiser pays the affiliate a commission or "success fee" based on the user's purchase. This is the defining characteristic of performance-based (affiliate) advertising.

### Step 3: Match to option

- a) Agree to participate → step (1)
- b) Click the advertisement → step (2)
- d) Purchase the desired product → step (4)
- c) Pay a success fee → step **(5)** ✓

### Answer: c) Pay a success fee on the basis of the sales of the product
