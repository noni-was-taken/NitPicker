# 2025A_FE-A_59 %% ex. 2025A_FE-A_83 %%

Which of the following is a financial statement that represents the assets, liabilities, and net assets of a company at a certain point in time and indicates the financial situation of the company?

a) Balance sheet
b) Income statement
c) Statement of Cash Flows
d) Statement of Changes in Equity
?
a) Balance sheet
### Explanation

The **Balance Sheet** (also called Statement of Financial Position) is a snapshot of a company's financial state **at a specific point in time**, showing:
- **Assets**: what the company owns
- **Liabilities**: what the company owes
- **Net assets (equity)**: the residual interest (Assets − Liabilities)

- **Income statement**: shows revenues and expenses over a *period* of time
- **Statement of Cash Flows**: shows cash inflows and outflows over a *period*
- **Statement of Changes in Equity**: shows changes in shareholders' equity over a *period*
