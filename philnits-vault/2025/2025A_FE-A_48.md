# 2025A_FE-A_48 %% ex. 2025A_FE-A_83 %%

In a business scenario where marketing managers analyze customer interests and sales patterns, design promotional campaigns, and then send targeted emails to customers, which of the following is a modeling technique used to represent how information flows between internal processes, data stores, and external entities?

a) Class Diagram
b) Data Flow Diagram (DFD)
c) Entity-Relationship Diagram (ERD)
d) Use Case Diagram
?
b) Data Flow Diagram (DFD)
### Explanation

A **Data Flow Diagram (DFD)** models how data moves through a system by showing:
- **Processes** (transformations of data)
- **Data stores** (where data is held)
- **External entities** (sources/sinks of data)
- **Data flows** (movement of information between components)

This matches the scenario of mapping information flow in a marketing business process.

- **Class Diagram**: models object-oriented structure (classes, attributes, relationships)
- **ERD**: models data entities and their relationships in a database
- **Use Case Diagram**: models functional interactions between actors and the system
