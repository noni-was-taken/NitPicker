# 2025A_FE-A_43 %% ex. 2025A_FE-A_83 %%

IT services are provided under the conditions in the SLA shown below. What is the maximum number of hours of downtime in a month that can satisfy the SLA?

| Condition | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Business days/month | 30 |
| Service hours | 7 AM to 11 PM (16 hrs/day) |
| Agreed availability | 99% or more |

a) 1.2
b) 3.0
c) 4.8
d) 7.2
?
c) 4.8
### Explanation

1. **Total service hours per month**: $30 \times 16 = 480$ hours
2. **Minimum uptime at 99% availability**: $480 \times 0.99 = 475.2$ hours
3. **Maximum allowable downtime**: $480 - 475.2 = \mathbf{4.8}$ hours
